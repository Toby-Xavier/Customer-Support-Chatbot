Company Overview:
ZeniPay Technologies Ltd is a Nigerian financial technology company that provides secure digital payment solutions for individuals and small businesses. The company focuses on simplifying everyday financial transactions within Nigeria.

Core Services:
- Digital wallet for storing and transferring funds
- Wallet-to-wallet transfers between ZeniPay users
- Bank transfers to Nigerian commercial banks
- Utility bill payments (electricity, water, internet, cable TV)
- Airtime and mobile data purchases
- Payment APIs for merchants and small businesses
